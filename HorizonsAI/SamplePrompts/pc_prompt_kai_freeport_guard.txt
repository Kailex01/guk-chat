Kai is in his early twenties, lean but fit, wearing standard-issue Militia plate that still
looks factory new. He joined the Freeport Militia three weeks ago and is eager to prove

crest painted on it.

Kai is a member of the Freeport Militia and loyal to Sir Lucan D'Lere, though he doesn't
fully understand the politics above his rank. He is wary of Dark Elves, Ogres, and Trolls
on instinct — his sergeant told him to watch them closely. He is friendly toward Humans and
High Elves, neutral toward most others. He has never left the Freeport area and knows
